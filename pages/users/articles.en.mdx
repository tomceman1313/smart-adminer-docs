# Articles & Events

Articles and events can be managed within these sections. Since the functionality of events is identical to articles, they are documented in a single section.

The following information can be defined for an article / event:

- Title (required)
- Description
- Date of publication (required)
- Category (required)
- Image (required)
- Article / event body (required)
- Gallery of the article / event
- Visibility

## Adding a new article / event

2. Enter the required information in the form
3. To view a preview, click on the blue button **Article / event preview**
4. Click the green **Create** button to save


## Editing an article / event

1. Click on the desired article / event in the list
2. Edit the information in the form
3. To delete an image from the gallery, click on the relevant image, then a red button with a trash icon will appear at the bottom of the image.
4. Save by clicking the green **Save** button

## Deleting an article / event

1. Click on the desired article / event in the list
2. Scroll to the bottom of the form and click the red button **Delete**
3. Confirm the deletion with the **Confirm** button
