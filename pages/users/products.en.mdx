## Adding a new product

2. Fill out the form (input fields, variants, parameters...)
3. Click the green **Create** button to save

## Product editing

1. For the selected product in the list, click the **Detail** button
2. Edit the product information to the desired status
3. Click the green **Save** button

## Product removal

1. For the selected product in the list, click the **Delete** button
2. Confirm the deletion by clicking the **Confirm** button

## Product variants

Each product must contain at least one variant. The variant is used to distinguish different variants of the product (it can be, for example, size or performance).

Individual variants can be added, edited, sorted and deleted within the available component.

<Callout type="warning" emoji="âš ">
   Any changes within the modification of variants are only saved by clicking the **Save** button at the end of the form
</Callout>


![Hello](/images/variant.PNG)

#### Adding a variant

1. Fill in the name, quantity and price fields
2. Click the green **Add** button

#### Modification of variant

1. Click on the desired input field of the variant you want to edit
2. Overwrite the value

#### Changing the order of variants

1. Click the button with the arrow pointing in the direction you want to move the variant within the order
